Our Story

Evoca Drinks was founded with a clear ambition: to create premium soft drinks without pricing them into a niche category.

From the outset, we committed to producing drinks we would genuinely choose ourselves. That meant refusing to compromise on taste, ingredient quality or production standards.

  • We would not produce fruit drinks without real fruit.
  • We would not rely on controversial colourings.
  • We would not use alcohol-based solvents in our formulations.

That commitment led to industry-first products and a brand built on integrity.

Built on Principle

Founded by Intisar Qasim and Asif Salahuddin - childhood friends since schooldays and with a passion to deliver the best - Evoca Drinks combines leadership experience with customer satisfaction.

Despite entering one of the most crowded markets in the world, Evoca has sold millions of cans to loyal customers by offering a credible, premium alternative!

Looking Ahead

With growing national distribution and continued product development, expect to see Evoca products on more and more shelves!

Our mission remains unchanged: deliver exceptional taste without compromise.
